Company overview

Nokukhandi Maintenance and Repairs is a company built from a vast array of experience. We provide full maintenance services and repairs programs tailored to your requirements, which can range from preventative maintenance (which is Daily machine inspection,Maintenance intervals, backlogs and Condition monitoring) on earth moving equipment. We are able to maintain all earth moving equipment (such as: - Off highway trucks, track type tractor (dozers), Graders, Loaders, Articulated trucks, Drills, lighting plants and Pumps), utilizing either our directly employed labour force or specialist sub-contractors who meet our requirements, both in terms of Quality Assurance and Health and Safety. Nokukhandi Maintenance and repairs is providing a quality service at competitive prices, together with the recognition of client’s requirements and provision of cost-effective solutions and high-level service delivery. It is able to offer personalised service which is highly valued by many customers. With the necessary procedures in place this will confirm that we are capable of providing bespoke services to the highest level and to the complete satisfaction of our client’s needs.
