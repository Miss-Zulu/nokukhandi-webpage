Our Services

General Maintenance and Repairs

We as Nokukhandi specialize on maintenance and repairs on all earth moving equipment such as: Caterpillar (CAT), Bell, Komatsu, Hitachi, etc., these repairs can be done on customer sites or in our own workshop for repairs such as Component replacements or scheduled repairs that take prolonged times.

We provide labour services on the following disciplines:

  • Diesel mechanics
  • Auto electricians
  • Boilermakers/ Welders
  • Rigger
  • Artisan assistants
  • Industrial cleaners
  • Storeman
  • Administration

At the commencement of every new contract, we carry out an indepth maintenance survey of the equipment and plant within your premises. This knowledge informs our maintenance solution for your business. All of our services can be provided under a Planned Preventative Maintenance programme, specifically designed to ensure continuity of service of your critical machinery, or under a response maintenance package. This will be done while complying with all Health and Safety Processes and Procedures. Nokukhandi has employees that are qualified in using Asset Management Systems such as SAP, AMT, CRM to work with the client in ensuring that the maintenance records are up to date.

Outsourcing and supplying of machinery (off-highway trucks, ADTs, Dozers, Louder, Excavators, Graders, Tipper trucks and Compactors).

General Supply (PPE, Stationery, Consumables)

Supply of Maintenance Spares (Components, Parts and Tools)

  • Conditioning Monitoring
